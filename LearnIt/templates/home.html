The System

HOW IT WORKS

01
Sign Up → 300 Points

Create your account and receive 300 points instantly. Build your profile and enter the arena.

02
Post Your Problem

Upload your Django code with a description. Spend points to post — keeps things quality, no spam.

03
Community Solves It

Other developers submit solutions in the comments. You review them all and choose the best one.

04
Reward the Winner

Award at least 10 points to your chosen solver. Points flow from you to them — real stakes.

05
Zero Points? Solve First

Ran out? You can't post until you earn them back. Solve problems, gain points, stay in the game.
